Have you ever heard someone complain on holiday that "this beer just tastes way better back home"? Perhaps you have heard big beer drinkers heavily critique a beer that you actually quite like. Perhaps you have been puzzled abroad by odd choices of beers.

Have you ever wondered why this is?

Our culture and geographical background heavily influence our perception of the world in general [1], but (much more importantly) also influence both our preferences for beer, and also how we rate and discuss them. In this data story, we take you on a journey through these geographical differences to better understand why they exist at all.
